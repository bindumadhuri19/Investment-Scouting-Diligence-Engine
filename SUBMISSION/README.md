## 📊 What This System Does

**Investment Scouting & Diligence Engine** - An AI-powered platform that automates investment research by:

✅ Fetching real-time financial data (Yahoo Finance, SEC EDGAR)  
✅ Calculating risk metrics (Altman Z-Score, debt ratios, liquidity)  
✅ Analyzing market sentiment (100+ news articles, keyword analysis)  
✅ Generating AI-powered recommendations (BUY/HOLD/SELL with confidence scores)  
✅ Providing actionable insights with red flags and opportunities  

**Built in 4 hours** with production-ready architecture and real data integration.
